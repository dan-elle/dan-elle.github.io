About me.

Hello World!

I'm a first-year student in the computer engineering program at the University of Toronto planning to specialize in the software stream. Currently, I work in HTML, CSS, Python, and C. I have a bit of experience in using MATLAB and am learning how to work in JavaScript. After graduating, I hope to work in software, potentially in cyber security or software development.

As a student, I am constantly looking for ways to get involved in my school community. This year, I was selected to be the Events Coordinator for the First Year Council (affiliated with EngSoc at the University of Toronto). I also participated in a design team during my first year called Robots for Space Exploration (RSX) where I learned about how my programming experience can be applied to more physical projects, such as the rover that is being built by the team.

Outside of programming, I love spending time learning new hobbies and playing instruments. My current favourite hobby is crocheting and my favourite instrument is the violin. I also enjoy photography. All of the pictures posted on this site were taken by me!
