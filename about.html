About me.

Hello World!

I'm a second-year student in the computer engineering program at the University of Toronto planning to specialize in the software stream. Currently, I work in HTML, CSS, Python, C, and C++. I have a bit of experience in using MATLAB and am learning how to work in JavaScript, Verilog, and C#. After graduating, I hope to work in software, potentially in cyber security or software development.

As a student, I am constantly looking for ways to get involved in my school community. This year, I was selected to be the Academic Director for the Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) club at the University of Toronto. I am also involved in the chapter of the Institution of Electrical and Electronic Engineers at the University of Toronto as a logistics associate.

Outside of programming, I love spending time learning new hobbies and playing instruments. My current favourite hobby is crocheting and my favourite instrument is the violin. I also enjoy photography. All of the pictures posted on this site were taken by me!
